Search and replace


Multiline Search
================
To do a multiple search in Sublime just hit ctrl-F then
paste block of text into the search box.

Hit ctrl + Enter to append additional lines to search text.

Repeatedly click Enter to continue finding the next
matching block of text.


Find and replace current tab
============================
ctrl + F



Find and replace globally (all files and in all directories)
============================================================
ctrl + shift + F
