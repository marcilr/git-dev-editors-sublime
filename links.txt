Exclude files and folders from search in Sublime Text 3
From Hanh Bui Thu Dec 14, 2017
On Sublime Text 3, go to the top bar Sublime Text > \
Preferences > Settings User (it might be slightly
different in Windows) and add the following lines:
"binary_file_patterns": ["*.jpg", "*.jpeg", "*.png", "*.gif", "*.ttf", "*.tga", "*.dds", "*.ico", "*.eot", "*.pdf", "*.swf", "*.jar", "*.zip"],
"folder_exclude_patterns": [".svn", ".git", ".hg", "CVS", "tmp"]
http://lugolabs.com/articles/90-exclude-files-and-folders-from-search-in-sublime-text-3
